Professional Training

The virtual environment is widely used in the industry as a training tool. For example, pilot training uses a flight simulator to train the pilot to handle some unexpected situations. The use of the virtual environment is used as an abstraction as the real world. We can use it to investigate situations that might be dangerous in the real life. Pilot Training

The virtual environment is built as a part of the flight simulation. This is aim to give the pilot a sense of immersion so that the pilot will have more experience before piloting a real jet or a plane. The flight simulation usually has two or three monitors to output a virtual environment. This is due to the fact in the real life, the pilot will only have two or three windows to observe the outside environment. The simulation also has the same set up as real aircraft for the pilot to have more experience with the controls

Healthcare

Being a surgeon can be very stressful and every move they make need to be very precise. Therefore a surgeon needs to practice a lot. With a virtual environment, new surgeons can have a sense of surgery before they have an operation and they can practice their skills and techniques through immersing in the virtual environments. This helps the new surgeons to ease their stress and they can a deeper understanding of the operation protocols. Virtual environment not only used in surgery, it is also used to treat Post Traumatic Stress Disorder(PTSD). The patients are given a virtual reality headset and it will display various images of the source of their disorder. The doctor can find out what will trigger the patient’s disorder. Also, the patient while the images are displayed, the patient will also learn some relaxation skills to learn to adapt their disorder.

Military

Virtual environment has become an important part of the combat training. Nowadays, new generation recruits are more familiar with games and play them very often in their leisure time. Using a virtual reality headset allows recruits to quickly adapted the military life and the combat zone as the headset is fully immersive. The recruits will have more experience with combat zone and learn to handle various situations. The recruits will learn how to make a safe decision and interact with the environment in the combat zone in a more controlled setting. Medics are also trained in this way, as they need to deal with various injuries and adapted combat zone environment. Using a virtual environment helps the trainee medics to handle a more complex medical situation in controlled environments.
