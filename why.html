Why VECG matters



Virtual environment is used in fields like vehicle-testing, plane-testing, formula 1 training etc. The goal is to make the virtual environment as realistic as possible to train the future pilots and drivers or to study their behaviour in those situations. Also VE is used in fields like Surgery and Medical Imaging. The main points of the virtual environment machines in this field is get data from the complex 3D structures, convert them into data which can be analysed by specialists and then project them onto 2D viewing surfaces such as computer screens.

VR is also used in the rehabilitation medicine where the creation of certain environments and situations help the patients to learn and carry out complex exercises in a more efficient way. One of the main spheres where Virtual Environment and Graphics have been implemented is Entertainment, and more precise Gaming Industry with 32.4 million citizens, only in the UK, interacting with a huge range of games every year. The goal of building and working on Virtual Environments and Graphics in this field is to cause the player to feel as excited as possible while gaming. This is done by implementing high-level graphics in the projects.
